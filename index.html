Who I Am

About Me

I am Collins Ogbuju, a final-year Industrial and Production Engineering student at Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka, graduating August 2026. I build things: mechanical models, data dashboards, websites, brand identities, and written narratives. The medium changes. The standard stays the same.

My engineering core is in SolidWorks with over 20 mechanical designs including gear assemblies, hinges, and production components. I speak the language of tolerances, GD&T, and engineering drawing. Alongside that, I work fluently in Python, SQL, Excel, and Power BI, turning raw operational data into decisions leadership can act on.

Beyond the engineering, I have spent over five years in graphic design, brand identity, and digital creation, and I ghost-write for Tech, AI, and Business founders globally. Published on Medium at @collinsogbuju.

I am actively targeting graduate trainee and entry-level roles in Nigeria's oil and gas sector, particularly Seplat Energy and ExxonMobil. Lean Six Sigma White Belt certified. SPE Nigeria Student Member.
